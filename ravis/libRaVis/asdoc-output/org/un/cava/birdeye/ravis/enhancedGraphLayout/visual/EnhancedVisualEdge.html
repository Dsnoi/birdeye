ImplementsIControllableVisualEdge, IVisualEdge, mx.core.IDataRenderer

A visual edge is just a representation of a graph edge in the VisualGraph context. However, right now the only feature a VisualEdge has is a reference to its Graph Edge and a data object to support it's drawing features. There is a small possibility that this class will be removed.

centered:Boolean  [read-write]

Property to indicate if the node should be centered around it's geometrical center (i.e. the center of it's view) or if it's origin would be the usual upper left corner. The value will be applied by the commit() and refresh() methods.
